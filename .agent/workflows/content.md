# /content - Content Creation Workflow

Follow this workflow to create marketing content.

## Step 1: Content Brief

Define the content requirements:

1. **Type**: What content are we creating?
   - Blog post
   - Social media content
   - Email copy
   - Landing page
   - Video script
   - Lead magnet

2. **Goal**: What should it achieve?
   - Traffic/SEO
   - Engagement
   - Leads
   - Sales
   - Education

3. **Audience**: Who is this for?

4. **Keywords**: SEO targets (if applicable)

## Step 2: Research

1. **Topic research**: Gather information
2. **Competitor analysis**: What exists?
3. **Keyword research**: SEO opportunities
4. **Audience insights**: What do they need?

## Step 3: Outline

Create content structure:

1. **Hook**: Opening that captures attention
2. **Main points**: Key content sections
3. **Examples**: Supporting evidence
4. **CTA**: What action to take

## Step 4: Write

Draft the content:

1. **First draft**: Get ideas down
2. **Headlines**: Multiple options
3. **Body copy**: Clear and compelling
4. **CTA copy**: Action-oriented

## Step 5: Optimize

Polish the content:

1. **SEO optimization**: Meta, headers, keywords
2. **Readability**: Shorter sentences, formatting
3. **Conversion**: Strong CTAs
4. **Voice**: Brand consistency

## Step 6: Publish

1. **Format**: Platform-appropriate
2. **Schedule**: Optimal timing
3. **Distribute**: Cross-promote
4. **Track**: Monitor performance

## Output

- Finished content piece
- SEO metadata
- Distribution plan
- Performance tracking
